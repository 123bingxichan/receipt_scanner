# Product Notes

## Audience

People who want a lightweight way to keep personal receipt records, understand where money goes, and preserve proof of purchases.

## MVP

- Receipt upload.
- OCR extraction.
- Review screen for corrected fields.
- Receipt list with search and filters.
- Manual expense creation.
- Spending dashboard by month and category.
- CSV export.

## Later Features

- Mobile camera capture with image cleanup.
- Recurring merchant/category rules.
- Budget targets.
- Email receipt forwarding.
- Shared household accounts.
- Warranty and return-window reminders.
- Attach receipts to imported bank transactions.
